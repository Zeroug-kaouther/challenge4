Recipe 1:

Margherita Pizza

  • Ingredients: Pizza dough, tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, fresh basil leaves, olive oil, salt
  • Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  • Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface.
  • Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  • Tear fresh mozzarella into pieces and distribute evenly over the sauce.
  • Bake in the oven for 10-12 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  • Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  • Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with a pinch of salt before serving.

Recipe 2:

Pepperoni Pizza

  • Ingredients: Pizza dough, tomato sauce, shredded mozzarella, pepperoni
  • Preheat oven to 475°F.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes.

Recipe 3:

Veggie Supreme Pizza

  • Ingredients: Pizza dough, tomato sauce, shredded mozzarella, bell peppers, red onions, black olives, mushrooms, spinach
  • Preheat oven to 475°F.
  • Spread sauce on dough, add cheese and veggies.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes.

Recipe 4:

BBQ Chicken Pizza

  • Ingredients: Pizza dough, BBQ sauce, shredded mozzarella, cooked chicken, red onions, cilantro
  • Preheat oven to 475°F.
  • Spread BBQ sauce on dough, add cheese, chicken, and onions.
  • Bake for 12-15 minutes.
